Timeline


The timeline of this fictional universe can basically be divided into four eras (bear in mind that the timeline is rather condensed. A lot more could be added). Some of the years have been converted between C.E (current era), G.E (galactic era), and F.E to give a better understanding of the time-scale. Unfortunately, it can only be an approximation infered from the various texts since a complete timeline with definite years has never been published.
